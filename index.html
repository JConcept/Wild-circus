FLAM'N'CO DIABOLO

The entry of the Flam'n Co troupe on flamenco rhythms will enchant you. The Chinese diabolo or yo-yo is a child's play that moves into an artistic form. With a skillful precision, the diabolos are turned, thrown and thrown all around the body, which makes them almost animated. The two dance together. An evolution that intensifies crescendo, a universe in which mix scenic choreography and agility. The two protagonists of this issue are French: Jean and Morgane.

Adelina Diane

From the stage of the famous show the biggest cabaret of the world to the trail of the Wild circus there is a step that crosses many artists, in particular this Italian duo. They propose beautiful evolutions combining tango and hulla-hoop playing between lightness and atypical rhythm, a beautiful universe evocative and poetic.

Get ready for an exotic moment, this duo is going to bluff more than one on frantic rhythms with the colors of the world they handle choreography, magic, theatrical techniques and special effects that they restore in a breathtaking performance of virtuosity, mastery of the Gestures and whose aesthetic invites to the escape of the senses...

The acrobats of the company Navas

Jumps more perilous than the others and even triple perilous jumps ! The troop of Navas, young prodigies Franco-Ecuadorian make human pyramides and trampoline until you take your breath away !

The equestrian show « They shoot angels, don’t they ? » (elegies)

In a unique space–is it the bottom of a volcano ?
– Bartabas returns on stage and delivers us a fascinating equestrian and poetic musing. For this new creation, he comes down again in the arena like a archangel fallen from his purgatory.

Because here the sky of the angels announces of dry land and the surges aim to be cavalier. Even the paradise of horses is not an easy one anymore.
To which messenger to dedicate itself, when the circle of hell does not stop being reversed and the seraphs in wings bashed up to reconquer their kingdom ?
In which star to trust when we walk on a thread over volcanoes ?
What assumption to believe when the punishment happens to the sound of church organs ?

With this new show, the thirteenth in thirty years, Wild circus pursues its indomitable, equestrian and poetic quest, in the unknown. The laughter often also ambles in this pit of chimeras reinvented each time, when whitefaced clowns looklike butchers stir on popular airs.

The great lions

The great lions, are unanimously considered the most beautiful and majestic specimens on the track. In spite of a perceptible ferocity, they will awaken the emotion on the track! Note in the group Meknes and its 320kg, all trained and presented by Steve Falck.
